about me

–––––––––

I'm a recent graduate who is interested in using her passion for digital rights, media and social justice to design technology and services that better serve marginalized communities.

New to the civc tech field, I want to use my strong background as a social science and humanities researcher to inform innovative and creative technology.

In my free time, I host a bi-weekly WHPK 88.5 FM radio show called OurSpace, a show that aims to explore technology and internet culture at large, and how the intersections of gender, race, class, and sexuality inform the way we engage with it.
