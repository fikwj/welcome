Electronica Frontier Alliance Activism Toolkit

While interning at the Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF), I conducted research to assess the needs of the Electronic Frontier Alliance (EFA): a network of over 90 grassroots groups promoting digital rights across the country. EFA groups ranged from academic research institutions, to established non-profits and neighborhood advocacy groups. To inform my assessment, I analyzed survey data and conducted in-depth interviews with EFA group leaders; I found that certain EFA groups had difficulty maintaining consistent membership and institutional memory. All of this research culminated in the toolkit linked above.
